cc.factorie.variable

Domain

trait Domain extends AnyRef

The "domain" of a variable---a representation of all of its values, each having type 'ValueType'. This most generic superclass of all Domains does not provide much functionality. Key functionality of subclasses: DiscreteVectorDomain have a dimensionDomain:DiscreteDomain, providing a size and DiscreteValue objects. DiscreteDomain extends DiscreteVectorDomain, having single value members: DiscreteValue; it is its own dimensionDomain. CategoricalDomain provides a densely-packed mapping between category values and integers.
