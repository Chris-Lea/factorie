ConllChainNer

class ConllChainNer extends DocumentAnnotator

A finite-state named entity recognizer, trained on CoNLL 2003 data. Features include context aggregation and lexicons. Trained by online stochastic gradient ascent with an L1 prior that leads to ~90% sparsity. Inference by Viterbi. Achieves ~91% field F1 on CoNLL 2003 dev set.
